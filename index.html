About me

I am an energetic person who takes a creative approach to my work.

As a hearing care professional at Amplifon, I have used non-standard methods of fitting hearing aids many times in order to achieve the best result for my client. At work, I showed my ability to manage time by planning visits of new and existing customers so as to have time to help current hearing aid users and achieve sales goals when meeting new customers. I have repeatedly solved the problems of new and existing hearing aid users by thoroughly analyzing the circumstances in which there were problems with using hearing aids and introducing appropriate solutions. I also had the opportunity to demonstrate my creativity and ability to work in a group while designing introductory materials for new employees in the company.
